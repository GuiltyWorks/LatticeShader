A Shader that draws a grid on a flat object such as Quad.
It can also be stained glass style.

[How to Use]
1.Apply the UnlitLattice material or StandardLattice material to the object
2.Select Lattice Mode
3.Adjust the parameters
  Lattice: Number of lattices
  Lattice Width: Width of lattice
  Lattice Rotation: Turn grid on / off
  Stained Glass: Resolution of Stained Glass
  Transparency: Stained Glass Transparency
  Lattice Color: The color of the lattice
  Square Color: Color other than grid



[Assumed Operating Environment]
Unity 2017.4.15f1 (64-bit)
